Step 1: Enter State of Ihram

Before reaching Miqat boundary, perform Ghusl (ritual bath), wear Ihram garments (two white unstitched cloths for men, modest dress for women), and make intention for Umrah by saying:

لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ عُمْرَةً

"Labbaik Allahumma Umratan" (O Allah, I answer Your call to perform Umrah)

Important: Avoid prohibited acts like cutting hair/nails, using perfume, hunting, or intimate relations.

Step 2: Recite Talbiyah

Continuously recite Talbiyah from Miqat until you begin Tawaf:

لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ لَبَّيْكَ، لَبَّيْكَ لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ لَبَّيْكَ، إِنَّ الْحَمْدَ وَالنِّعْمَةَ لَكَ وَالْمُلْكَ، لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ

"Labbaik Allahumma Labbaik, Labbaik La Sharika Laka Labbaik, Innal Hamda Wan-Ni'mata Laka Wal Mulk, La Sharika Lak"

Step 3: Perform Tawaf

Upon reaching Masjid al-Haram, perform 7 circuits around the Kaaba starting from the Black Stone:

  • Men should uncover right shoulder (Idtiba) during Tawaf
  • Begin each circuit facing Black Stone saying "Allahu Akbar"
  • Walk briskly (Ramal) in first 3 circuits for men
  • Recite prayers and supplications during Tawaf

Step 4: Pray at Maqam Ibrahim

After completing Tawaf, pray 2 rak'ahs behind Maqam Ibrahim (if possible) or anywhere in the mosque:

Recite Surah Al-Kafirun in first rak'ah and Surah Al-Ikhlas in second rak'ah

Step 5: Drink Zamzam Water

Proceed to Zamzam well area to drink water while standing, facing Kaaba:

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ عِلْمًا نَافِعًا، وَرِزْقًا وَاسِعًا، وَشِفَاءً مِنْ كُلِّ دَاءٍ

"O Allah, I ask You for beneficial knowledge, ample provision, and healing from every disease"

Step 6: Perform Sa'i

Walk 7 times between Safa and Marwah hills:

  • Start at Safa, end at Marwah (one complete round)
  • Men should run between green markers
  • Recite supplications and remember Hajar's search for water
  • Complete 7 rounds ending at Marwah

Step 7: Cut Hair (Taqseer)

Complete your Umrah by cutting or shaving hair:

For Men: Shaving entire head is preferred, or trimming at least 1 inch from all sides

For Women: Cut fingertip length from hair ends

Note: All Ihram restrictions are now lifted except intimacy with spouse
Umrah takes 2-4 hours to complete

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Umrah

General Questions

When is the best time to perform Umrah? +

Ramadan is the best time for greater spiritual rewards. For better rates and smaller crowds, we recommend after Moharram when prices are more affordable and the weather is pleasant.

How long does Umrah take to complete? +

The actual Umrah rituals can be completed in 3-4 hours. However, most pilgrims spend additional time for prayers and spiritual activities at the holy mosques.

Can I perform Umrah more than once during my trip? +

Yes! Many pilgrims perform multiple Umrahs. After each Umrah, you need to go beyond Makkah city limits and return for the next one. A common practice is: perform Umrah → go to Madinah → return to Makkah for second Umrah → depart from Jeddah.

Is there an age limit for performing Umrah? +

There is no specific age limit for Umrah. However, pilgrims should be in good health to perform the rituals. Elderly pilgrims and children should be accompanied by family members who can assist them.

Can children perform Umrah? +

Yes, children can perform Umrah. There is no minimum age requirement. However, children should be accompanied by parents or guardians, and parents should ensure the child's comfort and safety throughout the journey.

Booking & Packages

What's included in your Umrah packages? +

Our packages include: round-trip flight tickets, hotel accommodation in Makkah and Madinah, Umrah visa processing, and transportation between cities and airports.

How far in advance should I book my Umrah trip? +

We recommend booking at least one month in advance to secure the best prices and preferred hotels. For peak seasons like Ramadan, book 2-3 months ahead.

Can I customize my Umrah package? +

Yes! We offer customizable packages where you can select your preferred hotels, flight options, and duration according to your needs and budget.

What's the difference between Economy, Economy Plus, and Premium packages? +

The main differences are hotel distance from Haram, transportation categories, and room types. Economy has hotels with shuttle service, Economy Plus offers walking distance hotels, and Premium includes luxury hotels with private transportation.

Do you offer group discounts? +

Yes, we offer special discounts for groups of 5 or more people. Contact us for customized group rates and family packages.

Can I extend my stay in Saudi Arabia? +

No, extension is not allowed on Umrah visa. The visa validity is strictly for the duration of your Umrah package.

What happens if I miss my flight? +

If your flight ticket is non-refundable or non-changeable, you will bear the loss and need to pay for a new ticket. We recommend arriving at the airport 3-4 hours before departure.

Are there any hidden costs in your packages? +

No, we believe in complete transparency. All costs are clearly outlined in your package details with no hidden charges.

Visa & Documentation

What documents are required for Umrah visa? +

You only need a valid passport and recent passport-sized photographs. We handle all other documentation requirements.

How long does visa processing take? +

Visa processing typically takes 3-5 working days once we have all required documents.

Can women perform Umrah without a Mahram? +

Yes, women can perform Umrah without a Mahram. Saudi regulations now allow women of all ages to travel in groups without male guardians.

What are the passport validity requirements? +

Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months from your intended date of travel to Saudi Arabia.

Do I need any vaccinations for Umrah? +

Yes, pilgrims need polio and yellow fever vaccinations. We recommend consulting with your doctor for any additional health precautions.

Can I get a visa on arrival for Umrah? +

Yes, visa on arrival is available for American and British passport holders. However, we recommend obtaining your visa in advance for a smoother journey.

Accommodation & Transportation

How far are your hotels from the Haram? +

We offer both walking distance hotels and hotels with shuttle service. Our Economy Plus and Premium packages include hotels within walking distance, while Economy packages include hotels with regular shuttle services to the Haram.

Is transportation provided between Makkah and Madinah? +

Yes, all our packages include comfortable transportation between Makkah and Madinah.

How long is the journey between Makkah and Madinah? +

The journey between Makkah and Madinah is approximately 450 km and takes about 4-5 hours by road, depending on traffic and rest stops.

Is there wheelchair access at the holy mosques? +

Yes, both Masjid al-Haram in Makkah and Masjid an-Nabawi in Madinah have excellent wheelchair accessibility with ramps, elevators, and designated areas for people with disabilities.

Can I choose my hotel preferences? +

Yes, on custom packages you can select your preferred hotels based on availability and your budget requirements.
